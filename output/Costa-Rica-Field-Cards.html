Best forWorking dist.Flash comp

Best forWorking dist.Flash comp

  • Closer = more flash at same power

Mounting Order

1. Camera OFF → mount X3 on hot shoe
2. Turn ON X3 → turn ON camera
3. Turn ON AD100Pro (RX mode, same channel)
4. Attach ML-CD15 diffuser magnetically
5. Test fire: press X3 test button

TTL Mode (0.5x–1x on MkI)

Main screen should show Group A in TTL, FEC 0.0
Adjust: tap +/− for ±0.3 per tap, or slide the bar

Manual Mode (>1x, or always on MkII)

1. Tap group A display → enter single-group view
2. Tap TTL → toggles to M
3. Set power to 1/128 (tap +/− or slide bar)
4. Swipe right → back to main view
