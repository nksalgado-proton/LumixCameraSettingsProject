# Panasonic Lumix G9II Menu Settings - Comprehensive Options Guide

## Overview
This document contains detailed information about every menu setting available on the Panasonic Lumix G9II camera, including setting names, available options/values, and default values. Settings are organized by menu tab and section as they appear in the camera menu system.

## Video - Brightness & Coloring

### Luminance Level
- **Setting Name**: Luminance Level
- **Options**: [0-255] or [16-235], [16-255]
- **Default**: [0-255] for standard video
- **Notes**: Range changes based on [Photo Style] and [V-Log] settings

### Master Pedestal Level
- **Setting Name**: Master Pedestal Level
- **Range**: -15 to +15
- **Default**: 0
- **Availability**: Not available with [V-Log] or [REAL TIME LUT]

### Recording While Controlling Overexposure (Knee)
- **Setting Name**: Recording While Controlling Overexposure (Knee)
- **Sub-settings**:
  - **[AUTO]**: Automatic compression
  - **[MANUAL]**: Manual knee point and slope adjustment
    - **[POINT]**: Master knee point (80.0 to 107.0)
    - **[SLOPE]**: Master knee slope (0 to 99)
  - **[Off]**: Knee disabled
- **Default**: [AUTO]
- **Availability**: When [Photo Style] is set to [Like709]

### ISO Sensitivity (Video)
- **Setting Name**: ISO Sensitivity (Video)
- **Options**: AUTO, 100, 200, 400, 800, 1600, 3200, 6400, 12800, 25600
- **Default**: AUTO
- **Sub-settings when AUTO**:
  - **ISO Auto Lower Limit**: [100] or [400]
  - **ISO Auto Upper Limit**: [200]/[1200] or [200]/[3200]
- **Default Lower**: [100]
- **Default Upper**: [200]/[1200]
